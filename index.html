Western Ohio Junior Football Conference Pre-Game Announcement The WESTERN OHIO JUNIOR FOOTBALL CONFERENCE consists of 10 clubs from 10 cities and 5 counties in and around the Greater Miami Valley. The W.O.J.F.C. and its clubs would like to extend to each of you here today a warm and cordial welcome. All of the members of the WESTERN OHIO JUNIOR FOOTBALL CONFERENCE abide by high standards of conduct, competition and relations with our members. The athletes playing in this game are friendly rivals. While play my be fierce, all in attendance here today are reminded that this is a game between children and good sporting behavior is expected from everyone in attendance. The officials for this game have been selected and assigned according to procedures adopted by the WESTERN OHIO JUNIOR FOOTBALL CONFERENCE.
